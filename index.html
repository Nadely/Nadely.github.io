Hello Baby !

From Bump to Baby and Beyond: One App for Every Step.

"Discover a new experience in pregnancy monitoring! Our application accompanies you every step of the way on this wonderful journey. Get personalised advice thanks to AI, follow your baby's development week by week, and stay connected with other mums-to-be by exchanging ideas. Enjoy a worry-free pregnancy, thanks to our dedicated application."

Pregnancy monitoring !

Making appointments, asking questions, talking to other people... Everything you need in a single Application.

Monitoring your child's progress

Baby's here, the appointments are as present as ever, as is the need to talk, but the monitoring of size, weight and quantity of bottles is also becoming important.

Team

Developed by Kevin Pellissard and Nadège Tettelin, students at Holberton School France, Laval

This project was born following the arrival of the first Holberton baby in October 2023. This student's partner used two different applications to monitor her pregnancy and that of their child. So why not make 2 in 1 ?
